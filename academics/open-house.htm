2015 Open House

Congratulations on Your Admission to the Institute of Fine Arts!


We invite you to attend our 2015 Open House for Accepted Students on March 30-31. We hope that you will come see everything that the Institute of Fine Arts has to offer.
 
The Open House begins with The Great Hall Exhibition Opening Reception on Monday, March 30th from 6:00-8:00PM.  The Great Hall Exhibitions are curated by current students and presented in the expansive Great Hall of the James B. Duke House.
 
On Tuesday, March 31 we welcome you to visit with current students, learn about our research and teaching from current faculty, and explore special opportunities in conservation and digital projects.
 
Please let us know if we can welcome you at our Open House by RSVPing by March 26.
