Alumni

Alumni of the Institute of Fine Arts are working in leading institutions worldwide. The Institute has conferred more than 4,000 degrees since its inception. We are tremendously proud of our graduates who have gone on to be leading historians, archaeologists, conservators, art administrators, curators and writers. Our alumni are leaders in 43 countries and 97 cities. We are proud to promote the many accomplishments of our alumni on webpages like What to Read, which features recent publications by alumni, as well as, Alumni Achievements, which shares recent alumni appointments and awards..

Institute alumni enjoy an array of receptions, cultural outings and scholarly programs that are designed just for them. Groups such as the Alumni Association and the Recent Alumni Circle make it fun and easy to engage with the Institute. Programs such as IFA Mentors, the Alumni Fund and the IFA Legacy Society provide opportunities to give back and support those students following in alumni footsteps. Life at the Institute continues far beyond graduation. 

If you are an Institute alumna/us and would like to learn about ways to become involved with your alma mater, contact Andrea Yglesias, Alumni Affairs Officer at andrea.yglesias@nyu.edu or 212-992-5812.
