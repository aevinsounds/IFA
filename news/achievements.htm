Alumni Achievements

The Institute of Fine Arts has a robust community of over 2,000 alumni, living in 43 different countries. Institute alumni hold positions in major museums, auction houses, galleries, publishing houses and universities in New York City and beyond. We are proud to share some recent appointments and awards earned by our alumni over the past three years.

A more comprehensive list of alumni can be found in this interactive and searchable map. We would be pleased to add your recent accomplishment to that list. Please email us at ifa.alumni@nyu.edu and let us know what you are up to!
